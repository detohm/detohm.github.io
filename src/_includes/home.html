About Me

I'm a full-stack developer with almost 10 years' experience. I worked on various types of platforms for both corporates and startups. My interests are software architecture, software engineering, and product development for large-scale of users.

About my professional experience, I was a founding engineer and CTO at FINNOMENA. I helped build the platform from prototyping until its growth stage as a Series-B-Funded startup with ~170K accounts and $950M+ in total assets.

FINNOMENA is a fintech startup for Robo-Advisor & Mutual Fund Trading Platform. It serves mutual fund brokerage business including Account Opening, Trading, Payment, Settlement, and Portfolio Recommendation.

In the earlier days, I developed the prototyping, designed the architecture, iterated the product features, set up cloud infrastructure and deployment, etc.

The company grew so fast. We have about 50+ engineering staffs when I left the company and I'm so proud of them :)

Prior joining FINNOMENA, I used to build my own startup name “CashGrow”. Even though it's already failed, we won the national startups competition during that time. We had opportunity to have a business trip to Seoul, Korea where I visited lots of awesome startups and people there.

Before joined startups, I worked with Kimberly-Clark - The personal care corporation that produces consumer products such as Huggies, Kleenex, Scott, etc. I had an opportunity to lead a project which relocated me to US for a couple months to work with business team.

I also worked with Thomson Reuters (Refinitiv - LSEG now) as performance engineer. I performed performance testing on entreprise systems and identified the root causes of the issues.

I graduated with a second-class honours degree from Chulalongkorn University in Computer Engineering.
